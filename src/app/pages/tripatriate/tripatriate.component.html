WHEREAS:-
a: KPC owns and/or operates and maintains in various places in Kenya a system of pipeline storage and ancillary facilities (hereinafter referred to as the System ) for all kinds of petroleum products.
b: The OMC is a dealer in various grades of petroleum products and uses the System from time to time.
c: The OMC has requested and KPC has agreed to allow the OMC to use the System for receipt storage transportation and delivery of petroleum products owned by the OMC or otherwise financed by the Financier at the request of the OMC
d: KPC and the OMC have entered into Transport and Storage Agreement "the TSA" dated 2nd May 2019 and the OMC has agreed to assign their rights over the financed consignments to the Financier pursuant to clause 18.2 of the TSA
e: This Agreement Reference. TRINITY/KCB/ MT STI SUPREME EX GULF ENERGY LTD/PMS / 400MT/25-2024 TRANSIT shall be read in conjunction with the Transportation and Storage Agreement "TSA"
f: The Financier has agreed to provide financing to the OMC to facilitate acquisition of Consignments of petroleum products for which the OMC has provided security to the Financier. It has been agreed by the parties herein that for purposes of day to day operations, the OMC will have control and ownership rights over the products financed by way of security, subject however to the terms of this Agreement. Provided that KPC shall hold the first irrevocable rights to recover amounts due to KPC from the OMC presently or in future in respect of the transactions and services rendered by KPC to the OMC, as the case may be, on all the products held in custody by KPC for the account of the OMC.
g: Subject to clause (f) above, it is a term of the arrangements that petroleum products financed by the Financier from time to time and held on account of the OMC with KPC hereinafter called the Petroleum Products shall at all times be deemed to be owned by the Financier as security for amounts advanced and accordingly the Financier shall alone authorize their disposal.
h: The OMC has now requested and KPC has agreed to allow the OMC to have the petroleum products financed by the Financier in KPC s custody and any of its rights arising thereunder or in connection with the TSA signed between KPC and the OMC, to be used as collateral for such financing in accordance with the terms hereinafter appearing.
A. AGREEMENT
1.
The OMC hereby confirms to KPC and to the Financier that:
1.1
The Petroleum Products held on account of the OMC with KPC that are the subject of this Agreement have been financed by the Financier and are accordingly to be held by KPC or by the OMC in trust for the Financier, subject to the provisions of this Agreement
1.2
The Petroleum Products are to be disposed of in accordance with the written instructions of the Financier as provided in this Agreement
1.3
KPC shall charge the OMC an administration fee of US Dollars 2,000 per month excluding VAT payable in advance. An additional 0.01% will be charged for Petroleum Products handled or stored during the quarter in excess of Hydrocarbon value of Dollars 1,000,000.
1.4
KPC shall in addition to the administration fee be reimbursed by the OMC, any costs or expenses incurred by KPC directly as a result of carrying out its obligations under this Agreement
2.
KPC hereby acknowledges the confirmation by the OMC and further confirms to the Financier that it shall hold the Petroleum Products on account of the OMC but in trust for the Financier and shall at all times proceed as per the Financier s instructions.
B. OBLIGATIONS OF KPC
1.
Transportation and Storage Agreement:
1.1
To comply with all the obligations of KPC under the TSA
1.2
Upon receipt of any release order by the Financier, to give the Financier stock statements of the Petroleum Products held by or delivered to the System on account of the OMC and financed by the Financier
2.
Trust and Statements
2.1
Subject to the provisions of this Agreement, to hold the Petroleum Products on account of the OMC but in trust for the Financier to the intent that the ownership of the Petroleum Products shall at all times be deemed to vest in the Financier as the beneficial and/ or legal owner AND KPC hereby acknowledges that the Petroleum Products shall at all times be held by KPC in trust for the Financier.
2.2
To release the Petroleum Products only as per the release orders signed by duly authorized officers of the Financier from time to time as provided for in this Agreement.
2.3
To advise the Financier in writing of the grade and quantity of the Petroleum Products held in trust for the Financier and to ensure that such statements are accurate and reflect the stock balances on the account.
2.4
KPC shall send an advance copy of the statement by email to the Financier followed by a hard copy duly stamped and signed by the authorized officials duly appointed and communicated to the Financier in writing, by KPC and it is hereby agreed that a statement issued by KPC in accordance with the terms of this Agreement shall be deemed to be final and conclusive and a true reflection of the products held by KPC save only for errors which have reasonably occurred beyond KPC s control.
3.
Third Party Holding Certificate
Upon receipt of the Financier s Notification as stated in clause D.1.2., KPC shall issue a Third Party Holding Certificate based on the final outturn data from the appointed independent surveyor determined after ship discharge.
4.
Indemnity To extend the indemnity provided to THE OMC under the Transportation and Storage Agreement to THE FINANCIER upon assignment of THE OMC s rights to THE FINANCIER.
5.
Insurance
5.1
To maintain at its own cost a comprehensive policy of insurance with insurers of repute: the liability of the KPC in respect of any act or default for which it may become liable to indemnify the OMC under the terms of this Agreement, and loss or damage of Petroleum Products held in trust for the OMC or the Financier as the case may be.
5.2
KPC agrees and covenants with the Financier that in the event that a claim becomes payable under any such policies for loss or damage to financed Petroleum Products, the Financier shall be paid first for any loss suffered by the Financier.
5.3
To provide to the Financier a copy of the policy of insurance taken by KPC and any renewal thereof from time to time.
